About Us

Nena is 100% women owned BBBEE company operating in South Africa. We are the Manufacturers and wholesalers of Nena sanitary pads which we sell in bulk quantities at competitive prices. We are a business to business dealer. However, we do cater for individuals and organisations who would like to buy to supply sanitary pads to schools and other entities for their Corporate Social Investment (CSI) initiatives.

Our Product

Nena is a thin, highly absorbent unscented sanitary pad with wings. It is designed to fit for comfort and is tightly secured to ensure super absorbency.
